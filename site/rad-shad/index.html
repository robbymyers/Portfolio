Rad Shaddy

Energy Drink — 30 Second Commercial

3D Motion Branding Blender After Effects

Project Overview

I designed and produced a 30-second 3D spot for Shadrachs Coffee to launch their energy drink, Rad Shaddy.

In a saturated energy drink market, the creative approach focused on setting the product apart by emphasizing movement, adventure, and everyday momentum.

The van symbolizes freedom and mobility, reinforcing the idea that Rad Shaddy fits seamlessly into both daily routines and spontaneous moments. Everything was modeled, lit, and rendered in Blender, with editing and compositing finished in After Effects.
