Shadrachs Coffee — 3D Social Spot

3D Motion Social Media Blender After Effects

Project Overview

I designed and produced a 3D social spot for Shadrachs Coffee's VIP program, a limited time promotion offering loyal customers exclusive discounts and perks.

Because VIP cards are sold on a single day each December and redeemed throughout the following year, the messaging needed to communicate both value and urgency clearly and immediately.

The final piece was designed for social media, balancing concise communication with visually engaging motion to capture attention and drive action within a short window.

All assets were modeled and rendered in Blender, then edited and composited in After Effects.
