Project Overview

Drawing has always been the foundation of my design practice shaping how I observe, think, and create.

While my work evolved into digital and brand-focused design, the introduction of tools like the Apple Pencil and Procreate allowed me to reconnect with that foundation – combining the expressiveness of hand-drawn work with the precision of digital production.

Today, I apply this approach to create custom illustrated stickers for a range of clients, blending raw, hand-drawn character with clean, production-ready execution.

This ongoing practice strengthens my ability to think visually, iterate quickly, and bring a distinct, human quality into my broader design work.
