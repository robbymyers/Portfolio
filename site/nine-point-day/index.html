Shadrachs Coffee — 3D Social Spot

3D Motion Social Media Blender After Effects

Project Overview

I designed and produced a 3D social spot for Shadrachs Coffee's Nine Point Day, an annual promotion where customers earn nine loyalty points per purchase accelerating their path to a free drink.

The objective was to clearly communicate the value of the promotion while creating a visually engaging piece that could capture attention in fast-moving social feeds.

The final spot was optimized for social media, balancing clarity of message with motion-driven visuals to increase engagement and campaign visibility.

All assets were modeled, lit, and rendered in Blender, then edited and composited in After Effects.

Recognition

The project received two Gold ADDY Awards at the Northeast Arkansas ADDYs for Social Media and Motion Graphics.
